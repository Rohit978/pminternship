Profile Setup Guidelines

Profile Photo
Upload a professional headshot with good lighting and a clean background. Avoid casual photos, group pictures, or images with filters.
Professional Summary
Write a compelling 3-4 sentence summary highlighting your background, interests in product management, and career goals. Include relevant coursework or projects.
Skills Section
List both technical and soft skills relevant to PM roles. Include tools like Excel, SQL, Figma, Jira, and soft skills like analytical thinking and communication.
Experience & Projects
Include all relevant experience including internships, part-time jobs, personal projects, and leadership roles. Use action verbs and quantify achievements where possible.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

Don't leave sections incomplete, use unprofessional email addresses, or include irrelevant information that doesn't relate to product management or your target roles.

Working with Our AI Chatbot

Be Specific About Your Preferences
The more detailed you are about your preferences, the better the AI can match you. Specify preferred cities, company stages, team sizes, and work environment preferences.
Ask Follow-up Questions
Don't hesitate to ask the AI to explain why certain matches were suggested or request alternatives if the initial recommendations don't align with your goals.
Update Preferences Regularly
Your preferences may evolve as you learn more about PM roles. Regularly chat with the AI to refine your preferences and get updated recommendations.
Use Natural Language
Communicate with the AI as you would with a career counselor. Use complete sentences and provide context about your background and goals.

🤖 AI Best Practices

The AI learns from your interactions. The more you engage and provide feedback on recommendations, the better it becomes at understanding your preferences.

Application Process

Application Timeline
Most companies respond within 1-2 weeks. If you haven't heard back after 2 weeks, you can send a polite follow-up through our platform.
Cover Letter Tips
Customize each cover letter to the specific company and role. Highlight relevant experience and explain why you're interested in that particular company's products.
Required Documents
  • Updated resume (PDF format preferred)
  • Cover letter tailored to the role
  • Portfolio or project samples (if applicable)
  • Unofficial transcript (some companies)

⚠️ Application Do's and Don'ts

Do: Apply to 5-10 positions weekly, follow up professionally, research each company thoroughly.
Don't: Apply to every posting without customization, ignore company culture fit, submit generic applications.

Process Guidelines

Follow these guidelines for a smooth and successful registration and internship experience.

Youth Registration & Profile Creation

DOs

  • Read eligibility criteria carefully before registering.
  • Use your own active and valid mobile number & email ID.
  • Upload correct, up-to-date, and genuine documents.
  • Fill in all academic and personal details completely.
  • Use a clear and professional passport-size photograph.
  • Keep your login credentials safe and private.

DON'Ts

  • Never share your login credentials or OTP with anyone.
  • Do not provide incorrect or misleading information.
  • Avoid uploading blurry or unreadable document scans.
  • Do not leave any required sections of your profile incomplete.
  • Never use ID proofs or documents belonging to someone else.
  • Don't rush; double-check all details before final submission.

Internship Process

DOs

  • Read all internship guidelines and details carefully.
  • Submit your application well before the deadline.
  • Maintain professional and polite communication.
  • Provide progress reports on time as required.
  • Adhere to all assigned tasks and internship rules.
  • Complete your assigned tasks within the given timelines.

DON'Ts

  • Avoid late submissions of your application or reports.
  • Do not plagiarize or copy content from other sources.
  • Don't ignore constructive feedback from your mentors.
  • Do not skip mandatory orientation or review meetings.
  • Avoid unprofessional, careless, or rude behavior.
  • Do not misuse any resources provided during the internship.

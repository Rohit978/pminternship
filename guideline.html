Platform Guidelines

Your complete guide to using our internship platform effectively and making the most of your application journey.

Getting Started

1. Create Your Account

Register on our portal using a valid email address. A verification link will be sent to you to activate your account.

2. Complete Your Profile

Fill out all the sections in your applicant profile, including personal details, address, and academic information. A complete profile is crucial for getting noticed.

3. Build Your Resume

Use our AI-powered Resume Builder to create a professional resume. You can generate content and even the final design with AI assistance.

4. Explore Programs

Navigate to the "Explore Programs" page to see the various sectors offering internships. You will be prompted to log in before viewing the listings.

Profile Setup Guidelines

Accurate Information

Ensure all details, especially your name, date of birth, and academic marks, are entered correctly as they appear on your official documents.

Address Details

Provide your full and correct address. The dynamic State and City dropdowns are designed to help ensure accuracy.

Secure Password

Choose a strong password that is at least 6 characters long. Use the "show/hide" icon to verify your password as you type.

Using Our AI Assistants

AI Application Assistant

Our main chatbot can answer your questions about the application process, required documents, and technical issues. It uses Google Search to provide up-to-date information.

AI Resume Builder

The resume builder uses AI to help you write your professional summary and job descriptions. In the final step, it designs a complete, professional resume layout for you.

AI Recommendation Engine

Once you are logged in, our custom machine learning model will analyze your profile and recommend the most relevant internships for you on the homepage.

Application Process

Logging In

You must be registered and logged in to view internship listings. You can log in via the widget on the homepage or on the dedicated login page.

Browsing Internships

After logging in, you can navigate to the specific sector pages (e.g., Technology, Design) to view and apply for available positions.

Submitting an Application

Each internship listing will have an "Apply Now" button which will guide you through the submission process for that specific role.

Best Practices for Success

Keep Your Profile Updated

An up-to-date profile ensures our AI recommendation engine provides you with the most relevant internship opportunities.

Tailor Your Resume

Use the resume builder to create a strong base resume, but consider tailoring the summary and skills for each specific application to highlight the most relevant qualifications.

Be Professional

Ensure all your written communications, from your resume summary to any direct contact, are professional and free of errors.

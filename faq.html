Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform, the application process, and our AI-powered features.

How does the preference-based matching work?

Our AI-powered system analyzes your profile and preferences to match you with the most suitable internship opportunities. The more detailed your profile, the better the matches.

What kind of companies offer internships?

We partner with a diverse range of government ministries, departments, and public sector undertakings across various sectors like technology, research, and design.

How do I use the AI Application Assistant?

You can find the AI Assistant on its dedicated page. Simply type your questions about the application process, and it will provide you with helpful, real-time answers.

What is the typical duration of an internship?

Internship durations typically range from 2 to 6 months. The specific duration for each opportunity will be mentioned in the internship listing.

Are the internships paid?

Yes, all internships listed on our portal come with a stipend as per government norms. The exact amount will be detailed in the internship description.

What qualifications do I need?

Eligibility criteria vary by role but generally require you to be a student pursuing an undergraduate or postgraduate degree. Please refer to the specific internship listings for detailed requirements.

Can I update my profile after registration?

Yes, you can and should keep your profile updated. An accurate profile helps our AI recommendation engine suggest the most relevant internships for you.

How does the AI Resume Builder work?

Our resume builder uses AI to help you write content for your summary and experience sections. In the final step, it generates a complete, professional resume layout based on your inputs.
